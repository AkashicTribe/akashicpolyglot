Leads the Slavonic Computing Initiative, a volunteer group of developers seeking to expand computer support for Old Church Slavonic and modern Church Slavonic. Typefaces include Triodion, Pochaevsk, and Ponomar for modern CS; Shafarik and Monomakh for OCS and academic work; as well as Mezenets and other fonts for Znamenny Notation.
