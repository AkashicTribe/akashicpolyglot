Christian Mosmüller is a Vienna-based designer with a strong foundation in media technology and design. After completing his studies at a technical college specializing in media technology, he pursued a degree in Graphic and Information Design at the New Design University in St. Pölten, Austria.

During his academic journey, Christian discovered his passion for type design, culminating in a Bachelor thesis dedicated to this field. His work reflects a blend of technical expertise and creative vision, making a mark in the world of typography and design.
